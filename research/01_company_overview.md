## 1. Founding & History

- **Founded:** 1985
- **Founder:** Robert Walters (the individual), established in London, UK
- Originally founded as a specialist professional recruitment consultancy focused on accountancy and finance placements
- Listed on the **London Stock Exchange** in 2000 (ticker: RWA)
- Expanded aggressively through the 1990s and 2000s from UK-centric firm to global professional services group
- Built particularly strong presence in Asia-Pacific markets (Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia)

## 6. Robert Walters Outsourcing (RWO) — Distinct Business Unit

### What Is RWO?
RWO is a dedicated **professional outsourcing and managed services** business unit within the Robert Walters Group. It is distinct from both the core recruitment business and Resource Solutions.

RWO focuses on providing **outsourced professional functions and teams** to client organisations — a managed services / professional services outsourcing model rather than traditional staffing.

### How RWO Differs from Core Robert Walters & Resource Solutions

| Dimension | Robert Walters (Core) | Resource Solutions | Robert Walters Outsourcing (RWO) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Model | Permanent & contract recruitment | RPO/MSP for hiring functions | Managed outsourced teams/functions |
| Client relationship | Transactional / project hiring | Ongoing managed recruitment service | Ongoing managed operational service |
| Staffing type | Individual placements | Recruitment process management | Teams and functional outsourcing |
| Commercial model | Placement fees / contract margin | Management fees on recruitment | Fixed/variable managed service fees |
| Geographic focus | Global | Global | Primarily Asia-Pacific + selective global |

### RWO Service Lines (Preliminary — verify on live site)
1. **Business Process Outsourcing (BPO)** — finance, HR, administration, compliance, customer operations
2. **Professional Outsourcing** — professional-grade outsourced talent in volume (finance teams, legal support, compliance, tech teams)
3. **Shared Services Setup** — helping clients establish shared services centres, often in lower-cost APAC geographies
4. **Employer of Record (EOR) / Payroll Outsourcing** — legal employer for client workforces in markets without a legal entity
5. **Compliance and Regulatory Support Outsourcing** — particularly relevant in financial services
6. **Project-Based Outsourcing** — delivering defined project outputs through an outsourced team

### RWO Geographic Focus
Particularly active in **Asia-Pacific** markets:
- Malaysia (shared services and BPO hub)
- Philippines
- Singapore
- Hong Kong
- Japan
- Vietnam
