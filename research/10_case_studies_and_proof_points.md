**Client:** Unnamed leading European bank
**Challenge:** Hiring in a challenging environment where competitors offered significantly higher salaries

## 5. Types of Triggers That Lead to RWO Engagement

Based on case study analysis:
- **Digital transformation programmes** (Speedy Hire, KPMG, Harrods) — most common trigger
- **Volume hiring surge** (Speedy Hire — 100+ hires needed)
- **Specialist talent scarcity** (European bank — hiring despite salary disadvantage)
- **Geographic talent mapping** (Financial services — multi-location talent availability)
- **Previous agency dissatisfaction** (Speedy Hire — communication and feedback gaps)
- **Senior/niche hire urgency** (KPMG — tight deadline, exclusive mandate)
