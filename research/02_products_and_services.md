## 1. Three Core Service Lines (Group Level)

1. **Recruitment** — specialist professional recruitment (permanent, contract, temp, executive search)
2. **Recruitment Outsourcing** — RPO and MSP/Contingent Workforce Solutions
3. **Talent Advisory** — market intelligence, ED&I audits, workforce consulting, future of work

## 2. Recruitment Outsourcing — Service Models

### 2a. Full RPO
- Comprehensive outsourcing of the **entire hiring process** — sourcing, selection, assessment, offer management, onboarding
- Manages timescales, budgets, application volumes, compliance
- Delivery through a mix of **onsite, nearshore, or offshore** delivery teams
- Long-term, strategic partnership model (not transactional)
- **URL:** https://www.robertwalters.co.uk/our-services/recruitment-process-outsourcing/full-rpo.html

### 2b. Project RPO
- **Rapid response** to one-off or short-to-medium-term talent demands
- Operational within **6 weeks**
- No need to add headcount to client's internal TA team
- Use cases: scaling fast, opening new office, launching new product, bolstering talent in key area
- **URL:** https://www.robertwalters.co.uk/our-services/recruitment-process-outsourcing/project-rpo.html

### 2c. Modular RPO
- Blended approach — manages **specific components** of the recruitment process
- Acts as an extension of the client's TA function
- Modules available: candidate sourcing, shortlisting, assessment, interviewing, onboarding, admin
- Bespoke team built to fit within client's infrastructure
- **URL:** https://www.robertwalters.co.uk/our-services/recruitment-process-outsourcing/modular-rpo.html

## 3. Contingent Workforce Solutions (CWS) / MSP

### Managed Service Provider (MSP)
- Manages the **end-to-end non-permanent/contingent workforce** for clients
- Uses a **Vendor Management System (VMS)** for full visibility of spend, compliance, supplier management
- Vendor neutral — manages all staffing suppliers on client's behalf
- Full visibility of contingent workforce spend → better budget control
- Reduces hidden costs: long time-to-fill, inconsistent quality, administrative burden of managing multiple suppliers
- Acts as extension of client's team, aligned with company culture
- **URL:** https://www.robertwalters.us/our-services/outsourcing/managed-service-provider.html

### CWS vs RPO Distinction
- **RPO** = permanent hiring; provider manages full end-to-end recruitment cycle or specific parts
- **CWS/MSP** = non-permanent/contract resource delivery and management
- RPO = long-term hiring strategy; CWS = flexibility for shorter-term/project-based hiring

## 7. Commercial/Pricing Models (Industry Standard — RW does not publish specific rates)

Three models applied in the market (RW uses these structures):

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| **Management Fee** | Monthly/hourly fee covering staff, tech, sourcing, account management |
| **Pay-for-Performance (Cost Per Hire)** | Fee per successful hire; open fee + close fee per requisition |
| **Hybrid / Blended** | Monthly management fee + pay-for-performance; balances risk and cost predictability |

- Pricing is **bespoke per client** — not publicly advertised
- Geographic reach affects pricing (national vs global programmes cost more)
- Project RPO: operational within weeks; Full RPO: 1–3 months to fully integrate
