## 2. Strategic Context

### "Simpler, Better, More Balanced" Strategy (launched February 2024)
Barclays launched its most significant strategic overhaul since the financial crisis in February 2024. The three pillars are:
- **Simpler:** Reducing organisational complexity, standardising data and platforms, driving digital/AI adoption
- **Better:** Improving financial returns and customer experience through technology
- **More Balanced:** Capital discipline; reducing Investment Bank's share of Group RWA from high 50s% to ~50% by 2028

**Updated 2026–2028 Targets:**
- Group RoTE: >14% by 2028 (11% target for 2025; 12%+ for 2026)
- Capital distributions: >£15bn across 2026–2028
- Cost-to-income ratio: "high 50s" by 2026 (currently 67% in IB as of 2024)

### Cost Restructuring and "Project Minerva"
- £2bn total cost reduction target [Verified — CNBC, Feb 2024]
- 5,000+ roles eliminated since 2023; a further 2,100 cuts planned by 2026 [Verified — ainvest.com]
- £500m cost reduction target for 2025 alone [Verified — eFinancialCareers]
- **Offshoring drive:** Marketing, executive assistant, technology, and research roles being offshored to India; new Barclays India team replacing London roles [Verified — PeopleMatters.in, eFinancialCareers 2025]
- AI being used to identify roles for relocation to India (Feb 2026) [Verified — AML Intelligence]
- Technology jobs cut in January 2025 (~50 roles); further waves in June 2025 [Verified — eFinancialCareers]

### APAC Growth Agenda (Counter-Cyclical to UK/EU Cuts)
Barclays is simultaneously cutting UK/US headcount while aggressively expanding its APAC investment banking footprint:
- Multiple senior investment banking appointments in APAC in 2025: Paul Johnson (Head of Equities APAC, from Goldman Sachs), Jean-François Mastrangelo (Head of Markets APAC, Oct 2025), Joseph Lee (Head of High Touch Sales Trading APAC, from HSBC), Neel Laungani (Head of Financial Sponsors APAC), Avinash Thakur (Head of IB APAC)
- Corporate Bank APAC expansion: Vijay Shankar (Head of Global Transaction Banking APAC & ME) and Amaar Khan (CIO & Head of Corporate Banking Technology APAC & ME), both Feb 2025
- Hong Kong serves as the hub for China cross-border business, equities, and IB; active graduate programme for 2026
- Japan: Active CIB operations with recent equities hires
- Barclays aims to deliver >11% RoTE in 2025 from APAC growth

**Strategic HR/TA Implication:** Barclays is running a dual-mandate TA challenge — managing significant reduction-in-force and offshoring complexity in the UK and US while simultaneously scaling specialist investment banking headcount in Asia Pacific. This is precisely the structural pressure that justifies flex RPO capacity.

### M&A Context
No major M&A activity as at March 2026. The bank completed the acquisition of Tesco Bank (UK personal finance portfolio) in November 2024 — adding customers but not significant headcount complexity.
