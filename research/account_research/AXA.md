**2.1 AXA IM divestiture workforce impact**

The July 2025 sale of AXA Investment Managers to BNP Paribas is the most significant recent workforce complexity driver. AXA IM had approximately 2,800+ employees globally (Paris, London, Hong Kong, Singapore, Tokyo). The transition created:
- Integration/separation complexity for HR (offboarding IM employees, TUPE/transfer obligations in the UK, works council processes in France)
- Loss of the asset management TA pipeline — the P&C and Health TA teams now carry the full hiring load
- Residual workforce in AXA's finance, strategy, and operations teams who supported IM [Inferred from scale of transaction]
