## 1. Proprietary Technology Platforms

### RW Plus
- **What it is:** Robert Walters' own tech-enabled outsourcing solution
- **Technology:** Powered by **ethical AI and automation**
- **Structure:** Dynamic modules for each phase of the talent lifecycle (modular architecture)
- **Integration:** Integrates with client systems (flexible, not locked-in)
- **Key differentiator:** "Ethical AI" — responsible, transparent AI use; not black-box automation
- **URL:** https://www.robertwalters.co.uk/our-services/outsourcing/rw-plus.html

### RW Insights / RWIntelligence
- **What it is:** One-stop **market intelligence platform** for RPO clients
- **Capabilities:**
  - Real-time talent maps — availability and attrition by location/role
  - Pay benchmarks by market and job function
  - Gender diversity metrics
  - Competitor intelligence (salary competitiveness)
- **Proven outcome:** European bank client achieved **+36% hires per quarter** using RW Insights despite salary disadvantage
- **Best for:** TA leaders needing data to inform hiring strategy, not just execution
- Target user: HR/TA analytics-oriented buyers

### RWConnect

- Likely: client/candidate engagement portal, pipeline management, or real-time reporting dashboard

## 2. VMS (Vendor Management System) — MSP Technology

- Robert Walters uses **VMS technology** as part of its MSP solution

## 3. ATS (Applicant Tracking System) — RPO Technology

- Robert Walters offers **flexibility** on ATS: works with both in-house platforms AND third-party integrations
- Quote from SelectSoftware Reviews: "Unlike some competitors that keep everything in-house, Robert Walters mixes it up by allowing both in-house and third-party tool integrations, so clients can stick with what's already included in their RPO software or integrate whatever they are currently using"
- Common ATS platforms in their client ecosystem (inferred): Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Greenhouse, Taleo, iCIMS
- **Key insight:** ATS flexibility is a competitive differentiator vs. providers who mandate proprietary platforms

## 9. Competitive Technology Positioning

| Competitor | Technology Approach | RWO vs. Them |
|---|---|---|
| PeopleScout | Affinix — proprietary AI platform; very tech-led | RWO: more flexible/integrated; human expertise emphasis |
