## 1. Global Footprint Overview

- **31 countries** total group operations
- **23 countries** with client account teams (outsourcing-specific)
- **6 global service centres** supporting on-site outsourcing teams
- Talent delivery into **60+ countries** for outsourcing clients
- APAC generates the **majority of group revenue** — largest and most strategically important region

## 5. Global Delivery Model

### Service Centres
- **6 global service centres** that support on-site client teams
- Enable true global delivery — talent into 60+ countries
- Mix of:
  - **Onsite** — embedded in client's offices
  - **Nearshore** — service centres near client geography
  - **Offshore** — delivery from lower-cost locations (APAC delivery hubs)

### Key Offshore/Near-shore Hubs (Inferred)
- **Malaysia** — strong BPO/shared services market; RWO has significant presence
- **Philippines** — offshore delivery capability
- **Singapore** — regional management hub

## 6. Geographic Expansion Signals

### Where RWO is Growing (2024–2025 Signals)
- **SE Asia returning to growth** (+2% in 2024) — positive momentum
- **Japan** growing (+3%) — historically strong RW market
- **Mainland China** growing (despite broader Greater China softness)
- **EMEA** — new MD appointment signals investment intent
- **APAC** — dedicated MD appointment + COO attention = strategic priority

### Markets Under Pressure (2024)

- UK regions (-45%) — macro economic headwinds
