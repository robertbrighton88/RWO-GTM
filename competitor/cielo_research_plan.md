# Cielo Deep Dive — Research Plan

**Objective:** Produce comprehensive competitive intelligence on Cielo to equip RWO with sharp positioning, a practical sales battlecard, and a buyer decision framework.

**What we already know (from `competitive_positioning.md`):**
- Very large global scale (113 countries, 51 languages)
- Technology-first, data-driven positioning
- Strong US market presence; pure-play RPO specialist
- Primarily permanent RPO — less specialist sector depth than RWO
- US-centric heritage
- RWO counter: sector expertise and APAC depth

This plan goes 10x deeper.

---

## Research Workstreams

### 1. Cielo Service Architecture & Delivery Model
**Goal:** Understand exactly what Cielo sells and how they deliver it — service-by-service comparison against RWO.

**Research questions:**
- What are their exact RPO service tiers and delivery models?
- Do they offer MSP / Contingent Workforce Management or is it purely RPO?
- Do they have Project RPO / Modular RPO equivalents?
- How is delivery structured (onsite, nearshore, offshore)?
- What is their proprietary technology platform (CieloBridge or similar)?
- Do they have a talent advisory / consulting layer?
- What does their AI offering actually include vs. what is marketed?

**Sources:** Cielo website, solution briefs, case studies, webinars

---

### 2. Cielo Positioning & Messaging
**Goal:** Map their narrative and value propositions — so we can pre-empt, reframe, or counter in competitive deals.

**Research questions:**
- What is their primary positioning statement / headline claim?
- What is their "why Cielo" argument?
- How do they frame technology as their core differentiator?
- What pain points do they lead with in RPO conversations?
- Do they claim any specialist sector depth or is it purely volume-scale?
- How do they pitch against the market and against named competitors?
- What is their employer brand / candidate experience story?

**Sources:** Cielo website, thought leadership, LinkedIn, webinars, analyst summaries

---

### 3. Cielo Analyst & Award Standing
**Goal:** Know where Cielo ranks in every relevant analyst report — understand exactly how their credibility compares to RWO's Star Performer position.

**Research questions:**
- Everest Group RPO PEAK Matrix ranking — Leader, Major Contender, or Star Performer?
- Everest Group CWM/MSP ranking (if applicable)
- NelsonHall NEAT RPO positioning
- Gartner / SIA recognition
- Any recent analyst commentary specifically on Cielo strengths/weaknesses
- Awards in the last 18 months

**Sources:** Everest Group, NelsonHall, SIA, Gartner, Cielo awards page, press releases

---

### 4. Cielo Geographic & Sector Depth
**Goal:** Map where their 113-country claim is real versus thin — identify where RWO has genuine, defensible ground.

**Research questions:**
- Where are their actual delivery hubs and service centres?
- How strong are they in APAC specifically (Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia)?
- What sectors do they serve — is BFSI a genuine focus or just a claim?
- Do they have specialist financial services capability or just volume hiring?
- Are they genuinely global or primarily US + Western Europe?
- Where are their biggest and oldest client relationships?

**Sources:** Cielo website, LinkedIn offices, case studies by sector, news, analyst reports

---

### 5. Cielo Technology Stack
**Goal:** Understand their tech claims and compare against RWO's RW Plus / RW Insights.

**Research questions:**
- What is their proprietary platform (name, capabilities, positioning)?
- What ATS/HRIS integrations do they support (Workday, SAP, Oracle)?
- How do they position AI — matching, screening, analytics, market intelligence?
- Do they have a market intelligence / talent mapping product comparable to RW Insights?
- What third-party tech partnerships do they hold?
- How do clients describe their technology in reviews?

**Sources:** Cielo website, G2, press releases, tech partnership announcements

---

### 6. Market Perception — Voice of the Customer & Employees
**Goal:** Understand how actual buyers and candidates experience Cielo — find the gaps between marketing and reality.

**Research questions:**
- What do clients say on G2, Capterra, or HR forums?
- What are the most common complaints from Cielo clients?
- What do candidates experience working through Cielo programmes?
- What do Cielo employees say about delivery quality, culture, and operational standards?
- Are there notable client churns, public disputes, or SLA failures?
- What does the TA/recruiter community think of Cielo vs. RWO on LinkedIn?
- Do clients feel they get specialist expertise or a volume/process machine?

**Sources:** G2, Glassdoor, Indeed, LinkedIn, Reddit (r/humanresources), HR forums

**Goal:** Understand where Cielo is heading — new products, acquisitions, leadership, market moves.

### 10. Competitive Win/Loss Framework

**RWO wins when:**
- BFSI sector expertise is required (Cielo is generalist)
- APAC hiring is a significant part of the scope (Cielo is US-centric)
- Mid-to-senior specialist hiring is the core need (Cielo is built for volume)
- Client values quality of hire over throughput
- Advisory / market intelligence is part of the requirement (RW Insights)
- Relationship-led, long-term partnership model is important
- Candidate brand matters — Robert Walters name improves attraction in specialist markets
- Regulatory complexity (risk, compliance governance inside banks) is a feature of the role

**Cielo wins when:**
- High-volume, high-throughput permanent RPO at global scale is the requirement
- US or Western Europe hiring is the primary geography
- Technology platform is a primary evaluation criterion
- Client needs 50+ countries of consistent delivery
- Analyst credibility at the very top of the RPO market matters to procurement
