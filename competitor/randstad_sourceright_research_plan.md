# Randstad Sourceright Deep Dive — Research Plan

**Objective:** Produce comprehensive competitive intelligence on Randstad Sourceright to equip RWO with sharp positioning, a practical sales battlecard, and a buyer decision framework.

**What we already know (from `competitive_positioning.md`):**
- Backed by Randstad Group (one of the world's largest staffing companies)
- Strong MSP capability; global scale; Total Talent Management positioning
- Launched AI-driven Talent Innovation Center (May 2024)
- Predictive workforce analytics; RPO + MSP combined
- Randstad brand is generalist staffing — can undermine specialist positioning
- RWO counter: stronger brand in specialist/professional candidate markets; Randstad wins on breadth

This plan goes 10x deeper.

---

## Research Workstreams

### 1. Randstad Sourceright Service Architecture & Delivery Model
**Goal:** Understand exactly what Randstad Sourceright sells and how they deliver it — service-by-service comparison against RWO.

**Research questions:**
- What are their exact RPO, MSP, and Total Talent Management service tiers?
- Do they offer Project RPO / Modular RPO equivalents to RWO's flexible models?
- How does their Total Talent Management work in practice — is RPO and MSP genuinely integrated?
- How is delivery structured (onsite, nearshore, offshore)?
- What is their Talent Innovation Center — what does it actually deliver for clients?
- Do they have a talent advisory / consulting layer separate from RPO/MSP execution?
- What is their Relevate technology platform and what does it include?

**Sources:** Randstad Sourceright website, solution briefs, case studies, webinars

---

### 2. Randstad Sourceright Positioning & Messaging
**Goal:** Map their narrative and value propositions — so we can pre-empt, reframe, or counter in competitive deals.

### 10. Competitive Win/Loss Framework

**RWO wins when:**
- BFSI specialist expertise is required (Randstad Sourceright is generalist)
- APAC hiring is a significant part of the scope (RWO has deeper on-the-ground presence)
- Quality-of-hire and mid-to-senior specialist roles are the priority
- Client values the Robert Walters candidate brand over a generalist staffing parent
- Advisory and real-time market intelligence are part of the requirement
- Regulatory and compliance complexity inside banks requires navigational expertise
- Client is mid-sized enterprise, not a 50,000-person conglomerate needing global breadth

**Randstad Sourceright wins when:**
- True global scale and Total Talent Management in one contract is the requirement
- Client already has a Randstad Group staffing relationship to leverage
- Contingent workforce management is the primary (not secondary) requirement
- AI analytics and workforce planning at scale are the buying criteria
- Volume hiring in Europe or the US is the primary geography
