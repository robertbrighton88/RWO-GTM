## One-Line Summary

**Korn Ferry RPO:** Premium assessment-led RPO for senior and leadership hiring — built on executive search heritage, proprietary talent analytics, and the most recognised brand in executive talent.

**RWO:** Specialist BFSI-sector RPO and MSP — built on 40 years of operational delivery across all grades in financial services, with deep APAC regional expertise and integrated permanent + contingent workforce capability.

## Dimension-by-Dimension Analysis

### 1. Leadership Assessment and Talent Analytics

**Korn Ferry wins clearly.** Their proprietary assessment frameworks (Leadership Architect, Intelligence Cloud, Pay and Workforce Intelligence) are genuine intellectual assets built from decades of executive search and organisational consulting. No competitor — including RWO — matches Korn Ferry at the strategic analytics and leadership assessment layer.

**RWO's response:** Acknowledge the strength, then reframe scope. "Korn Ferry's assessment capability is excellent for senior leadership hiring. But an RPO programme spans far more than leadership levels. For your BFSI enterprise — compliance analysts, operations specialists, technology risk professionals, treasury teams — you need delivery at scale across all grades, not just a leadership assessment layer applied to the top 5% of roles. RW Insights gives your TA leadership market intelligence on all of those talent pools. We cover the full spectrum."

---

### 2. Senior and Leadership RPO

**Korn Ferry has the edge** for purely senior/leadership-focused RPO mandates. Their brand, assessment integration, and advisory capability align naturally with Director, VP, and C-suite hiring.

**RWO holds strong** in BFSI senior hiring, particularly where the roles require sector-specific credibility: risk management, compliance, investment operations, regulatory affairs. The Robert Walters brand attracts candidates in these specialist BFSI disciplines in a way that Korn Ferry's exec search reputation does not always extend to mid-senior specialist professional roles.

---

### 3. Volume and Operational RPO

**RWO wins clearly.** Korn Ferry's model is not built for high-throughput, all-grades operational RPO. Clients with hundreds or thousands of mid-professional hires annually alongside their senior hiring will find Korn Ferry's model creates bottlenecks and disproportionate cost at operational grades.

RWO's delivery model spans the entire hiring range — from graduate to senior professional to leadership — with consistent sector knowledge and delivery infrastructure across all tiers.

---

### 4. BFSI Sector Expertise

**RWO wins at the operational delivery level.** Korn Ferry has genuine BFSI expertise through decades of exec search in banking and financial services. But their RPO delivery expertise — understanding how to hire compliance specialists, risk managers, treasury operations professionals, and technology-for-finance teams inside the governance structures of regulated institutions — is less developed than RWO's, which has been built through operational delivery inside banks for 40 years.

---

### 5. APAC Regional Depth

**RWO wins clearly.** Korn Ferry's APAC presence is real for exec search but thinner for RPO delivery at professional and operational levels. Japan is the clearest example: hiring in Japan requires Japanese-language recruiters, on-the-ground networks, and cultural understanding of the Japanese labour market that Korn Ferry's exec search infrastructure does not deliver at the professional hiring levels relevant to RPO.

---

### 6. MSP and Contingent Workforce

**RWO wins by default — Korn Ferry does not compete here.** Korn Ferry RPO is a permanent hiring solution. They do not document MSP or contingent workforce management capability. For BFSI enterprises with both a permanent professional hiring requirement and a significant contractor/specialist contingent population, Korn Ferry cannot be the integrated partner. RWO can.

---

### 7. Pricing

**RWO is more competitively priced** for RPO at operational and mixed grades. Korn Ferry carries a significant price premium — reflecting their exec search brand, assessment integration, and advisory depth. For senior-only mandates where the premium is justified by the assessment value, this is fair. For broader, all-grades RPO programmes, Korn Ferry's pricing model creates friction.

---

### 8. Both Are Listed Companies

**Parity on ownership structure.** Unlike PE-backed competitors (Cielo/Audax), both Korn Ferry (NYSE: KFY) and RWO (part of Robert Walters plc (LSE: RWA)) operate within publicly listed, financially transparent structures. Neither has a meaningful advantage here.

## Where Each Provider Wins

### RWO Wins When:

- **Volume or mixed-tier RPO** across operational, professional, and senior grades is required — Korn Ferry is not optimised for this
- **MSP / Contingent Workforce Management** is part of the scope — Korn Ferry cannot compete
- **APAC hiring** is significant — particularly Japan, Singapore, Malaysia — RWO's on-the-ground RPO infrastructure is decisively stronger
- **BFSI operational depth** is required below MD/Director level — compliance analysts, risk professionals, treasury, technology — RWO's sector knowledge spans all grades
- **Budget sensitivity** is a factor — Korn Ferry's premium pricing structure is a barrier for operational-grade programmes
- **Candidate attraction via professional brand** in APAC matters — Robert Walters is better known than Korn Ferry for mid-to-senior specialist roles
- **Relationship and partnership continuity** matters — TIARA Long-Term Partnership Award 2024
- **Integrated talent advisory + delivery** at RPO-market pricing — RWO's Workforce Consultancy service vs. Korn Ferry's premium advisory layer

### Korn Ferry RPO Wins When:

- **Senior and leadership hiring** is the primary RPO scope — Director, VP, C-suite — where assessment integration adds the most value
- **Leadership assessment and succession planning** are buying criteria alongside RPO delivery
- **Talent analytics and Pay Intelligence** are valued as part of the engagement
- **The Korn Ferry exec search relationship already exists** in the account — expanding from exec search to RPO is a natural commercial extension and RWO does not have the same entry point
- **One firm for exec search + leadership development + RPO** is the preferred procurement model
- **Advisory depth** — workforce intelligence, skills frameworks, succession calibration — is a primary requirement rather than operational delivery at scale
