# Korn Ferry RPO Deep Dive — Research Plan

**Objective:** Produce comprehensive competitive intelligence on Korn Ferry RPO to equip RWO with sharp positioning, a practical sales battlecard, and a buyer decision framework.

**What we already know (from `competitive_positioning.md`):**
- Executive search heritage; strong leadership assessment capability
- Talent analytics, leadership advisory integration
- Premium brand; advisory depth
- Higher cost; more limited volume/operational RPO capability
- Senior-level assessment; analytics and reporting
- RWO counter: stronger in specialist mid-market and outsourced delivery at scale; natural overlap in senior hiring

This plan goes 10x deeper.

---

## Research Workstreams

### 1. Korn Ferry RPO Service Architecture & Delivery Model
**Goal:** Understand exactly what Korn Ferry RPO sells and how it differs from their executive search heritage — service-by-service comparison against RWO.

**Research questions:**
- What are their exact RPO service tiers and delivery models?
- Do they offer MSP / Contingent Workforce Management or is it primarily permanent RPO?
- Do they offer Project RPO or Modular RPO?
- How does their leadership assessment capability integrate with RPO delivery?
- How is delivery structured (onsite, nearshore, offshore)?
- What is their proprietary RPO technology platform?
- What is their talent advisory layer — how does it interact with RPO execution?
- How do they handle volume operational hiring vs. senior specialist roles?

**Sources:** Korn Ferry website, RPO solution briefs, case studies, webinars

### 10. Competitive Win/Loss Framework

**RWO wins when:**
- Volume or mixed-tier RPO (not just senior) is required
- MSP / Contingent Workforce Management is part of the scope (Korn Ferry is weak here)
- APAC hiring is significant (RWO has deeper regional presence)
- BFSI specialist depth is required at operational (not just leadership) level
- Budget sensitivity is a factor — Korn Ferry RPO is perceived as premium-priced
- Client wants an integrated RPO + advisory partner without paying exec search rates
- Speed and agility matter — Korn Ferry is process-heavy at senior tiers

**Korn Ferry RPO wins when:**
- Senior and leadership hiring is the primary RPO scope
- Leadership assessment, succession, and talent analytics are buying criteria
- The Korn Ferry executive search relationship already exists in the account
- Client wants one firm for exec search, leadership development, and RPO
- Data-heavy, analytics-led talent strategy is the primary need
