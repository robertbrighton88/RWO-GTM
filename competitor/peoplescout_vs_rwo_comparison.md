## One-Line Summary

**PeopleScout:** Technology-first, high-volume RPO with a proprietary platform (Affinix) and integrated employer brand capability — built for large-scale enterprise permanent hiring in North America and the UK.

**RWO:** Specialist BFSI-sector RPO and MSP — built on 40 years of specialist professional hiring expertise, APAC depth, and integrated permanent + contingent workforce capability for financial services enterprises.

## Dimension-by-Dimension Analysis

### 1. Technology — Affinix vs. RW Plus + RW Insights

**This is PeopleScout's primary pitch weapon.** Affinix is a genuine, mature, purpose-built talent acquisition platform. In a technology specification comparison, Affinix will score strongly against RW Plus on individual feature dimensions — particularly mobile-first candidate experience, AI matching, and analytics dashboards.

**RWO's response:** "Affinix is built to optimise volume throughput — faster screening, higher candidate volume, lower cost-per-hire at operational grades. RW Plus is our AI-powered delivery engine. But our distinctive technology differentiator is RW Insights — real-time external talent market intelligence that gives your TA leadership data on talent pool depth, salary benchmarks, and competitor hiring activity in your specific BFSI markets. Affinix answers 'how do we fill roles faster at volume?' — RW Insights answers 'what is the talent market doing and how should our strategy respond?' Those are different questions."

---

### 2. Volume vs. Specialist Quality

**This is the defining dimension of this comparison.** PeopleScout's model is optimised for high-volume, consistent delivery of operational and mid-level permanent hiring. RWO is optimised for specialist, relationship-led delivery of professional and senior roles in BFSI.

These are not competitive on the same ground. The question for any client evaluating both is: what does your actual hiring portfolio look like? If the majority of hires are volume operational roles, PeopleScout is better suited. If the majority are specialist professional roles in BFSI — compliance, risk, investment operations, treasury, senior finance — RWO is decisively stronger.

---

### 3. BFSI Sector Expertise

**RWO wins clearly.** PeopleScout serves financial services clients — but their financial services expertise is oriented toward operational and transactional hiring (contact centre, branch banking, insurance processing) rather than specialist professional BFSI hiring. The governance, regulatory, and operational knowledge required to hire inside a regulated financial institution at the professional level is not a PeopleScout capability.

---

### 4. APAC

**RWO wins decisively.** PeopleScout is a North American and UK-primary provider. Their APAC capability is thin and not a strategic priority. Robert Walters Japan, Singapore, and Malaysia are decisive competitive advantages for any BFSI client with significant APAC hiring.

---

### 5. MSP and Contingent Workforce

**RWO wins by default.** PeopleScout does not offer MSP. For BFSI clients with a significant specialist contractor population — which describes most large financial institutions — PeopleScout cannot be an integrated partner.

---

### 6. Employer Brand

**PeopleScout leads on employer brand capability.** The TMP Worldwide acquisition gives them a genuinely more developed employer brand and digital recruitment marketing capability than most RPO providers. If employer brand is a primary requirement alongside RPO, this is a real differentiator.

**RWO's response:** "For BFSI hiring in APAC, the most powerful employer brand is already built: Robert Walters. No EVP or digital campaign replicates 40 years of candidate trust in Japan, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Employer brand matters — but the best brand is the one candidates already recognise and respond to."

## When Each Provider Wins

### RWO Wins When:

- **BFSI is the primary sector** — specialist professional hiring at all grades
- **APAC is significant** — Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong
- **Specialist quality over volume throughput** — mid-to-senior professional roles where quality of hire is the measure
- **MSP / contingent workforce** is required alongside permanent — PeopleScout cannot offer this
- **Talent advisory and market intelligence** are part of the requirement
- **Mid-to-senior specialist hiring** where the Robert Walters candidate brand improves attraction
- **Long-term partnership** — TIARA Long-Term Partnership Award 2024

### PeopleScout Wins When:

- **High-volume operational and mid-level permanent hiring** is the core requirement
- **US-centric** — their strongest market by a significant margin
- **Technology platform (Affinix)** is a primary evaluation criterion
- **Employer brand and digital recruitment marketing** alongside RPO is required
- **Volume cost-efficiency** is the primary commercial driver — cost-per-hire at scale
- **Operational BFSI** — contact centre, branch banking, insurance operations — rather than specialist BFSI professional hiring
