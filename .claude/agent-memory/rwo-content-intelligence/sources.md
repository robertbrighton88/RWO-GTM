# Content Intelligence — Curated Source List

## Tier 1 — Industry Press (Highest Signal Value)

- **HRO Today:** hrotoday.com — Baker's Dozen RPO rankings, market analysis, practitioner interviews
- **UNLEASH:** unleash.ai — HR technology, future of work, enterprise talent strategy
- **HR Tech World:** hrtechworld.com — Conference coverage, vendor news, HR transformation
- **Personnel Today (UK):** personneltoday.com — UK-focused HR news, employment law, TA practice
- **People Management (CIPD):** peoplemanagement.co.uk — UK CIPD audience, senior HR practitioners
- **SIA (Staffing Industry Analysts):** sia.info — RPO/MSP market data, contingent workforce intelligence

## Tier 2 — Analyst Publications

- **Everest Group:** everestgrp.com — PEAK Matrix reports, RPO/CWM rankings
- **NelsonHall:** nelsonhall.com — RPO/MSP analyst reports and vendor evaluations
- **Gartner:** gartner.com — HR technology, workforce planning frameworks
- **Mercer:** imercer.com — Total Rewards, workforce trends, compensation data

## Tier 3 — Practitioner Thought Leadership

- **LinkedIn Pulse:** linkedin.com/pulse — First-person practitioner content (HR leaders, CHRO voices)
- **HRMI (HR Management Institute):** hrmi.com — Senior practitioner research
- **The Conference Board:** conference-board.org — C-suite HR research, CHRO surveys
- **Deloitte Human Capital:** deloitte.com/us/human-capital — Workforce trends, annual Global Human Capital Trends

## BFSI-Specific Sources

- **Financial News:** fnlondon.com — Banking, investment management, fintech talent
- **The Banker:** thebanker.com — Banking transformation, regulation, digital
- **Banking Technology:** bankingtech.com — Technology-led transformation in financial services

## Pharma & Life Sciences Sources

- **PharmaTimes:** pharmatimes.com — Pharma industry, talent, regulation
- **Fierce Pharma:** fiercepharma.com — Pharma news, M&A, workforce signals
