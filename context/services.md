# Robert Walters Outsourcing — Services

## Overview

RWO offers three core outsourcing service lines, plus an emerging advisory layer. Services are delivered through a mix of onsite, nearshore, and offshore teams — supported by 6 global service centres.

---

## 1. Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)

RWO manages all or part of a client's permanent hiring function. Three delivery models:

### Full RPO
- End-to-end management of the entire hiring process: sourcing → screening → assessment → offer → onboarding
- Manages timelines, budgets, compliance, and reporting
- Delivered via onsite, nearshore, or offshore teams
- **Best for:** Organisations with sustained, high-volume or complex hiring needs who want to fully transfer TA ownership

### Project RPO
- Rapid response to a defined, time-bound talent demand
- Operational within ~6 weeks
- No permanent headcount added to client TA team
- **Best for:** Growth spurts, new market entry, product launches, transformation programmes

### Modular RPO
- Manages specific components of the recruitment process only
- Acts as an extension of the client's existing TA function
- Client chooses which elements to outsource: sourcing, screening, assessment, admin, onboarding
- **Best for:** TA teams that want to augment capability without full outsourcing

---

## 2. Managed Service Provider (MSP) / Contingent Workforce Management (CWM)

RWO manages a client's contingent (non-permanent) workforce — contractors, temporary staff, freelancers, statement of work (SOW) engagements.

- Vendor neutral or preferred supplier model
- Spend visibility and compliance across all contingent categories
- Rate card management and benchmark data
- Performance analytics and workforce reporting
- **Everest Group Star Performer 2025** in CWM/MSP PEAK Matrix
- **Best for:** Organisations with significant contingent workforce spend seeking cost control, compliance, and visibility

---

## 3. Talent Advisory & Workforce Consulting

The fastest-growing service line — NFI up 51% in the UK in 2024.

- **Market intelligence & talent mapping** — where talent pools exist, competitor hiring activity, salary benchmarking
- **Workforce strategy consulting** — org design, workforce planning, skills gap analysis
- **ED&I advisory** — diversity audits, inclusion programme design, reporting
- **Employer brand consulting** — EVP development, candidate experience improvement
- **RW Insights platform** — real-time data on talent markets; used to drive smarter hiring decisions (case: European bank +36% hires per quarter)
- **Best for:** CHROs and CPOs who want strategic input on talent, not just execution

---

## 4. Technology Platform — RW Plus

RWO's proprietary AI-powered delivery platform, underpinning all service lines.

- Real-time hiring analytics and dashboards
- Integrates with client ATS and HRIS systems (Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, etc.)
- AI-driven candidate matching and screening
- Ethical AI framework — augments human judgement, does not replace it
- Diversity and pay benchmarking data
- **RW Insights** sub-module: market intelligence, talent mapping, competitor analysis
