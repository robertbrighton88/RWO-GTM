# Robert Walters Outsourcing — GTM Channels

## Overview

RWO's go-to-market is relationship-led and content-driven. They do not rely on outbound sales at volume or paid advertising. The primary channels are thought leadership, direct relationship development, events, and the strength of their recruitment brand creating inbound pull.

---

## 1. Thought Leadership & Content

The highest-volume and most consistent GTM channel.

**Annual Salary Surveys**
- Published globally across 30+ markets
- Major awareness and credibility tool — widely cited by HR and TA leaders
- Creates annual inbound engagement at scale
- Positions Robert Walters as a market intelligence authority

**Whitepapers & Reports**
- *Recruitment Outsourcing Insights* whitepaper — direct lead gen for RPO conversations
- Workforce trends reports, diversity and inclusion research, future of work content
- Targeted at CHRO, Head of TA, CPO audiences

**Blog & Insights Hub**
- Regular content on talent trends, hiring market conditions, RPO/MSP education
- Targets mid-funnel buyers researching outsourcing options
- URL: robertwalters.com/insights

**Webinars & On-Demand Content**
- "Understanding the Future of Work" — on-demand webinar series
- Used for lead capture and nurture
- Topics mirror macro narrative: flexibility, talent scarcity, technology

---

## 2. Direct Sales & Relationship Development

**Senior relationship-led sales**
- RWO's commercial model is built on senior-to-senior relationships — BD leaders targeting CHROs, CPOs, and Heads of TA at target accounts
- Long sales cycles (3–12+ months for Full RPO) require sustained relationship development
- Internal recruitment network acts as a warm referral engine — consultants passing intelligence on client talent pain to outsourcing BD

**Cross-sell from recruitment**
- Existing Robert Walters recruitment clients are the warmest pipeline for RWO
- A client using RW for specialist placement may be receptive to RPO when their hiring volumes grow
- The 2024 brand unification was partly designed to enable this cross-sell more cleanly

## 4. Digital & LinkedIn

**LinkedIn**
- Primary social channel for B2B GTM
- Content mix: thought leadership, salary survey data, award announcements, employee stories
- Leaders (Andrew Powell, Jessica Holt, Jenny Fulton) publish directly — personal brand as GTM channel
- Company page: Robert Walters (unified — post-rebrand)

**SEO & Website**
- robertwalters.com/our-services/outsourcing — core outsourcing hub
- Service-specific landing pages (Full RPO, Project RPO, Modular RPO, MSP)
- RW Insights as a content and lead-gen asset

**Paid / Programmatic**
- No evidence of significant paid advertising in their GTM mix
- Brand is strong enough to rely on organic and relationship channels

## 6. Partnerships & Ecosystem

**Technology Partnerships**
- Works alongside major ATS and HRIS vendors (Workday, SAP SuccessFactors) — not a direct partnership but integration capability
- Horsefly Analytics — talent intelligence data used within RW Insights platform
