# Robert Walters Outsourcing — Company Overview

## Who They Are

Robert Walters Outsourcing (RWO) is the outsourcing and managed services division of **Robert Walters Group** — a globally listed professional services and recruitment firm founded in London in 1985. The Group operates across 31 countries and is listed on the London Stock Exchange (RWA).

RWO is distinct from the core recruitment brand. Where Robert Walters places individual professionals, **RWO manages entire talent functions** — acting as a strategic partner that runs RPO, MSP, and workforce advisory programmes on behalf of its clients.

In 2024, the Group unified its brands — merging **Resource Solutions** (the RPO/MSP arm) into the Robert Walters brand. Everything now operates under a single, unified identity: **Robert Walters**.

## Business Structure

Robert Walters Group operates three interconnected capabilities under one brand:

1. **Specialist Recruitment** — permanent and contract placement across professional disciplines
2. **Recruitment Outsourcing** — RPO, MSP, and modular talent solutions (this is RWO)
3. **Talent Advisory** — market intelligence, workforce consulting, ED&I advisory

The outsourcing business is led globally by **Andrew Powell** (CEO, Robert Walters Outsourcing), with regional MDs for APAC (Jenny Fulton) and EMEA (Jessica Holt).

## Core Sectors

RWO's deepest expertise and strongest client penetration is in **Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI)**. This is the primary sector — the one where RWO has the longest track record, the most case studies, and the greatest competitive advantage. Other sectors are present but secondary.

- **Banking, Financial Services & Insurance (BFSI)** — Primary. Banks, asset managers, insurers, FinTech.
- Technology & Digital — Secondary
- Legal & Compliance — Secondary
- Pharma & Life Sciences — Secondary
- Professional Services — Secondary

## Geographic Priorities

- **UK** and **Asia Pacific** are the two largest and most established markets
- **EMEA** (ex-UK) and **US** are active growth markets

## Strategic Positioning

RWO positions as a **strategic talent partner** — not a staffing vendor. The ambition is to operate at C-suite level, advising on workforce strategy, not just filling roles. Their vision:

> *"To be the world's most trusted talent solutions business."*

Their purpose:

> *"To power people and organisations to fulfil their unique potential."*
